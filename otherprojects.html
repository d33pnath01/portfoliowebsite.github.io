Flox Don't Stop

A unique co-op experience where players race against the clock to reach as high as possible using blocks coming their way and collecting feathers to keep up their available block count.

Role: Producer, Level Designer and Gameplay Programmer
Technology: Unreal Engine 4, Blueprints, Perforce and Jira

Welcome to Earth

Play as a UFO escaping a secret facility in this puzzle platformer using a custom designer alternate controller. The controller was designed on an Arduino board and connected to Unreal Engine through APIs.

Role: Producer, Level Designer & Gameplay Programmer
Technology: Unreal Engine 4, C++, Blueprints, Perforce and Jira

Reaction

An iteration on the arcade classic - Asteroids. where the only way to move forward is from the recoil of the shooting mechanic. This game was built on a 2-week iteration using the Phaser3 Game Engine.

Role: Producer and Gameplay Programmer
Technology: Phaser3 , JavaScript, Atom Text Editor, Trello and Git

Spinning Space Cowboys

An arcade-style endless shooter where the only way to survive is to shoot at the endless horde of enemies. A week-long, 6-person project using the Pygame Engine as an introduction to game engines.

Role: Producer and Gameplay Programmer
Technology: Pygame Engine, Python, PyCharm, Trello and Git

Reef Rescue

A unique VR experience aimed at educating aquarium visitors on the impact of corals on the environment by playing the part of a coral reef restoration expert from the beginning of the process to the end.

Role: Producer, Designer & Gameplay Programmer
Technology: Unity VR, C#, Visual Studio, Jira and Git

Transience

A solo project made over a course of 4 weeks from mechanic and puzzle design to bug fixing. Basic concepts of shape language were applied in designing the levels with the constraint of minimalistic mechanics.

Role: Solo project
Technology: Unreal Engine 4 and Blueprints
